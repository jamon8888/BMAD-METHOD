# Founder Idea Validator Module

**Evidence-Based Business Idea Validation** through rigorous assessment of Market Demand, Founder Capability, and Distribution Feasibility.

## Overview

The Founder Idea Validator module provides a systematic, AI-powered framework for testing business viability across three critical pillars. In 30-45 minutes, get clear recommendations on whether to BUILD NOW, VALIDATE FIRST, PIVOT, or DON'T PURSUE your idea.

Built for founders, aspiring entrepreneurs, and innovators who need rapid, evidence-based validation before investing months building the wrong thing.

Based on the proven **Idea Validator** methodology by Avery Chernin.

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Getting the Most Value

1. **Be Honest and Specific**: Vague inputs = vague validation. Be specific about your idea, target market, and resources.

2. **Don't Skip Refinement**: Lisa's refinement phase ensures validation is testing the right thing. Don't rush it.

3. **Trust the Research**: Alex and Sarah conduct real research with sources. Trust evidence over optimism.

4. **Face Reality Early**: Better to pivot or stop early than after months of building.

5. **Use Validation Experiments**: If recommendation is VALIDATE FIRST, run the tests before building.

### Best Practices

- **Accept Fuzzy Starts**: All great ideas start unclear. Work with Lisa to crystallize.
- **Provide Context**: The more context you provide, the better the validation.
- **Question Assumptions**: Validators will challenge assumptions - this is valuable.
- **Act on Results**: Don't ignore validation findings. They save you time and money.
- **Iterate**: Most ideas need refinement, not rejection. Pivot and re-validate.

## â“ FAQ

### How long does validation take?
30-45 minutes for complete validation through all five agents.

### Do I need to talk to all five agents?
Yes for complete validation. But you can use individual agents (e.g., just Alex for market research) if you have specific questions.

### What if I get VALIDATE FIRST?
This is common! It means there's potential but uncertainties. Run the recommended validation experiments (usually 2-4 weeks) then reassess.

### What if I get DON'T PURSUE?
Trust the evidence. Better to know early than after months of building. Extract learnings and move to next idea.

### Can I re-validate after pivoting?
Absolutely! Pivots change assumptions. Re-run validation to test new direction.

### How accurate is the research?
Directional, not exhaustive. Validators spend 10-15 minutes per pillar researching with multiple sources. Sufficient for go/no-go decisions, not replacement for deep market research.

### What industries does this work for?
All. Framework applies to SaaS, e-commerce, services, marketplaces, content, physical products, and more.

### Is this for tech ideas only?
No. Works for any business idea: consulting practices, local services, online courses, apps, physical products, etc.

### Can teams use this together?
Yes! Team dynamics are specifically addressed in Marcus's founder alignment assessment.

### What happens after BUILD NOW?
You get a specific action plan with priorities, metrics to track, and next steps to execute.
